# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Analysis – Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset

## Dataset
- Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset (UCI ML Repository)
- Contains campaign data including visitors, leads, and customer conversions.

## Tools Used
- Python, Pandas
- Matplotlib / Seaborn
- Google Colab for analysis and documentation

## Objective
Analyze the marketing funnel:
- Visitors → Leads → Customers
- Identify drop-offs at each stage
- Calculate conversion rates
- Compare monthly performance
- Provide actionable recommendations

## Funnel Metrics
| Stage       | Users  | Conversion Rate | Drop-off Rate |
|------------|-------|----------------|---------------|
| Visitors   | 41,188 | —              | —             |
| Leads      | 5,625  | 13.66%         | 86.34%        |
| Customers  | 4,640  | 82.49%         | 17.51%        |

## Key Insights
- Major drop-off occurs at Visitor → Lead stage (~86%)
- Lead → Customer conversion is strong (~82.5%)
- Monthly performance varies; some months show significantly better conversions

## Recommendations
1. Improve Visitor → Lead conversion via optimized landing pages and CTAs  
2. Replicate high-performing campaigns and channels  
3. Use retargeting and personalized engagement to reduce drop-offs  
4. Track monthly funnel KPIs to guide data-driven decisions
